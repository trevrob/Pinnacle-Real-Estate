About Pinnacle

Brian McGinnis founded Pinnacle Real Estate LLC in 2001. Mr. McGinnis has extensive experience in hospitality functions, operations and development. As Director of Development for Westin Hotels & Resorts, Mr. McGinnis was responsible for marketing Westin’s hotel management, franchise and development services. His eleven years of experience at Westin includes market and financial feasibility studies, facilities planning and investment analysis for hotel and resort projects in North America and Asia.

In addition to his hospitality expertise, Mr. McGinnis gained valuable knowledge in the office arena as a Commercial Project Manager for Intracorp Seattle. He specialized in office development, land acquisition and sale agreements. He was also responsible for leasing and disposition of the 80,000 square feet Intracorp building as well as two industrial properties developed by Intracorp.

Prior to joining Intracorp, Mr. McGinnis worked as an Associate at Laventhol &Horwath, a national hotel and real estate consulting company. He conducted feasibility analysis and appraisals for a variety of development projects and convention facilities located in Washington, Oregon, Idaho and Alaska.

Mr. McGinnis graduated from Washington State University with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Hotel Administration and currently serves as Chair of the Advisory Committee for WSU’s School of Hospitality Business Management. For the past five years he has taught Hotel Development courses at Washington State University as well as the University of Washington. Brian was an honoree at the 75th Anniversary of WSU’s Hospitality Business Management Projectogram.
